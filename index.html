03 — Manifesto

The producer tag is a billboard on someone else’s song. I’d rather not.

Producer tags are a marketing decision that became a default. A loud whisper of who made the beat, stapled across the front of the track, optimized for SoundCloud discovery and stolen-loop arrests. They were never about the music.

notag ships the work clean. The artist can keep the front of their song. The labels can do A&R without my voice in their conference room. The fans can hear the record the way the artist wants it heard. If credit matters, the splits and the metadata carry it. The drop doesn’t.

Anonymity isn’t a gimmick here. It’s how the music gets to be the loudest thing on the file.
